What Is the Guzheng?

The guzheng is a traditional Chinese string instrument known for its expressive melodies and graceful sounds. With a history of over 2,500 years, it's a beautiful blend of art, culture, and emotion.

About Our Ensemble

The Guzheng Ensemble is more than just music. We nurture students with strong character, discipline, and confidence through weekly guided practice and public performances. From first plucks to graceful solos, every member grows together in harmony.
