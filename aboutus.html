"Our Story"

The Gu Zheng Club was founded in 2018 by a group of five students who shared a deep love for traditional Chinese music. It all began when one of them brought her guzheng to a school talent show — her performance left the audience in awe and sparked interest among her classmates. Inspired by the positive response, the group approached a music teacher and proposed starting a club where students could learn and perform together. With the school’s support and guidance from experienced players, the GuZheng Club was officially formed. Since then, we’ve grown into a passionate community of students who perform at school events, host workshops, and keep the art of guzheng alive and thriving.
