Still Have Doubts? Here Are Some Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the Guzheng Club? - The Guzheng Club is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting the appreciation and performance of the Guzheng, a traditional Chinese musical instrument.
  • How can I join the Guzheng Club? - You can join by filling out the form above or contacting us via email.
  • Do I need prior experience to join? - No prior experience is required. We welcome beginners and experienced players alike!
  • When does the club meet? - We meet every Thursday from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM in Music Room 3.
