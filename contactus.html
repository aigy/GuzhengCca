Here Are Some Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the Guzheng Club? - The Guzheng Club is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting the appreciation and performance of the Guzheng, a traditional Chinese musical instrument.
  • How can I join the Guzheng Club? - You can join by filling out the form above or contacting us via email.
  • Do I need prior experience to join? - No prior experience is required. We welcome beginners and experienced players alike!
  • When does the club meet? - We meet every Tuesdays and fridays from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM in Music Room 3.

Don't believe us? Just listen to some of our alumni's testimonies

"I joined the Guzheng Club in my first year knowing nothing about the instrument. The seniors and instructors were so patient, and soon I was performing in school concerts and even community events. That foundation led me to join a semi-professional Chinese orchestra after graduation, where I’ve since recorded music for cultural documentaries and performed at international showcases. The confidence I have today began right here.”
— Rachel Tan, Class of 2021

"Being part of the Guzheng Club was more than just learning music — it was where I found lifelong friends and a deeper appreciation for Chinese culture. Every performance taught me teamwork, discipline, and confidence. After graduating, I continued my passion for the guzheng and was invited to perform at the National Arts Festival in 2023. Even now, every time I play, I’m reminded of those afternoons filled with laughter and music.”
— Li Wen, Class of 2019
